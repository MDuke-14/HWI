<product_requirements>
The HWI Unipessoal time-tracking application offers comprehensive employee time management, including clock-in/out, detailed monthly reports (previously also Faturação), and overtime calculations. It supports PWA features, vacation/absence management with email notifications and admin approval. Admin users have elevated privileges for user, vacation, and manual time entry/editing (including entries crossing midnight), with automatic recalculations and email notifications for edits. User profiles now mandate a phone number. Recent updates enable month/year selection for reports, display individual time entries in history, and allow admins to access other users' history and reports directly. The latest major request is to develop a robust Sistema de Relatórios Técnicos, including client management, detailed report forms with dynamic technician entries, mileage tracking (x2 for round trip), work type selection (Diurno, Nocturno, Sábado, Domingo/Feriado), photo uploads with text descriptions, material tracking (used/budget), a Pedir Cotação workflow (with email selection and auto-generated email body), and digital/text signatures. The final report should generate a PDF link for email distribution to clients.
</product_requirements>
